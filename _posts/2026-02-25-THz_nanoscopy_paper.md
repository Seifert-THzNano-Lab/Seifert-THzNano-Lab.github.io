We show terahertz nanoscopy of spintronic photocurrents. A key step towards resolving ultrafast spin and charge dynamics on nanoscales. Special thanks to Felix Paries, Reza Rouzegar, Georg von Freymann, Daniel Mittleman and Xiaojun Wu. [Preprint @ ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.20960)
