Design Rationale

This design moves away from literal illustrations to a cleaner, symbolic representation of your research field.

  • 1
    The Hexagon (Nano):

    A split hexagonal bracket represents atomic lattices and nanostructures. It "holds" the physics in the center.

  • 2
    The Wave (THz):

    A red sine wave flows through the center of the structure, symbolizing the interaction of THz radiation with matter.

  • 3
    Subtle Motion:

    The wave flows continuously, and the atomic nodes breathe. This adds life without being distracting.
